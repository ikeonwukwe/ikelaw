1. No Lawyer–Client Relationship

Your use of this website, including submitting a consultation booking request or a contact form, does not create a lawyer–client relationship between you and Ike Law (Law Office of Ike Onwukwe), or any lawyer at the firm. A lawyer–client relationship is only formed once our office has expressly agreed to represent you, conflicts have been cleared, and a written retainer has been signed. Do not send confidential or time-sensitive information through this website until a lawyer–client relationship has been formally established.

2. Not Legal Advice

The content on this website — including practice area descriptions, blog-style content, and general information about immigration processes — is provided for general informational purposes only and does not constitute legal advice. Immigration law is complex and fact-specific; a general description of a program or process on this site may not reflect how the law applies to your individual circumstances, and immigration rules, policies, and processing practices change frequently. You should not act, or refrain from acting, on the basis of any information on this site without first seeking individualized legal advice from a licensed lawyer.

Ike Onwukwe is a lawyer licensed by the Law Society of Ontario (in accordance with Rule 4.2-1.1 of the Law Society's Rules of Professional Conduct, which requires that marketing materials disclose licensing status) and a licensed member of the Nigerian Bar Association. Nothing on this site should be taken as an offer to practise law in any jurisdiction where the firm or its lawyers are not authorized to do so.

Past results do not predict or guarantee similar outcomes in any other case, as every matter depends on its own facts, evidence, and the discretion of the relevant decision-maker.
